Economic Inequality Identifiers in City of Toronto Neighbourhoods, 2011

A series of factors related to the effects of income inequality - reliance on social assistance, risk of falling into debt, and crime (theft), compiled into an index by neighbourhood.

Eric Lawton, Ryerson University, February 11 2021, WGS 84. Data Sources: Toronto Wellbeing http://map.toronto.ca/wellbeing/ and Toronto Open Data https://open.toronto.ca/
